Privacy, explained clearly.

This notice explains what information Vulcet receives through the website, why it is used and the choices available to you. Last updated 24 July 2026.

ControllerProject enquiriesAnalyticsYour rights

Who is responsible

Vulcet is the independent digital studio responsible for the processing described here. Privacy questions and requests can be sent to hello@vulcet.com.

Project enquiries

When you submit the contact form, Vulcet receives the information you choose to provide, such as your name, work email, organisation, project type, budget, timing, website address and project description.

This information is used to assess and respond to your enquiry, take steps toward a possible contract and maintain necessary business records. Do not include sensitive personal information in the project brief.

The form is processed through Web3Forms. Its handling of submitted data is also governed by its own service terms and privacy practices.

Analytics and consent

Google Analytics is used to understand aggregate website use only after you allow analytics. Until then, analytics storage is denied through Google Consent Mode. Your choice is stored locally in your browser so the site can remember it.

You can change that choice at any time using the Cookie settings button on this page or in the footer. The site also loads typefaces from Google Fonts, which may involve a request to Google servers.

Retention and recipients

Enquiry information is kept only as long as reasonably needed to discuss the project, meet legal or accounting obligations, or resolve a dispute. Data may be handled by service providers required to operate the form, hosting and analytics. Transfers outside the European Economic Area may occur where these providers operate internationally and are handled using the safeguards offered by the relevant provider.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances and applicable GDPR rules, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction or portability of your personal data, object to certain processing, or withdraw consent. You may also complain to the relevant data protection authority.

To make a request, email hello@vulcet.com. Reasonable identity verification may be requested before fulfilling it.

Changes

This notice may be updated when the website, services or legal requirements change. The date above shows the latest revision.
